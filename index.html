Ideas Worth Spreading.


TEDxYouth@DPSMIS is a vibrant platform for young minds in Doha, Qatar, where ideas ignite and conversations flourish. Under the banner of DPS Modern Indian School, this esteemed event has been the cornerstone of youth engagement, fostering a community of thought-provoking individuals. This year's motif, "The Art of Flaws," transcends the conventional perception of imperfections, encouraging us to embrace our unique quirks and vulnerabilities as essential threads in the tapestry of life's richness and authenticity.
