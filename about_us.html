About Us

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading ,TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event,TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program,but individual TEDx events are self-organized.(Subject to certain rules and regulations.) TEDxYouth@DPSMIS is the longest running youth event in Doha, Qatar. It is organized by DPS Modern Indian School with an aim to provide a platform where ideas exchange and conversations ignite. This year’s theme, “The Art of Flaws” is a philosophical perspective that encourages individuals to perceive imperfections as intrinsic elements of human experience rather than deficiencies. It encompasses the acknowledgment and acceptance of one's own vulnerabilities, idiosyncrasies, and imperfections as essential components that contribute to the uniqueness and richness of life.
